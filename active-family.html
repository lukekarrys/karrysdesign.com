Active Family Chiropractic

A new location, a new name and a bigger vision! Along with all of that Active Family Chiropractic needed a new identity that embodied their mission of providing wellness for the entire family.

The energy of this chiropractic office is fun, family and sports oriented. We crafted a new identity around bright colors and active people to convey this spirit.
